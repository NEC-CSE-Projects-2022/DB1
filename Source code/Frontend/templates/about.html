About Our Research

Understanding Fingerprint Ridge Structures with Adaptive CNNs

Who We Are

The project “Learning Ridge Structures: adaptiveCNN-Based Local Orientation and Frequency Estimation for Fingerprints” is dedicated to advancing biometric research by leveraging deep learning. Our adaptive CNN dynamically learns ridge structures, enabling precise orientation and frequency estimation. This work is vital for fingerprint enhancement, authentication, and secure identification systems.
